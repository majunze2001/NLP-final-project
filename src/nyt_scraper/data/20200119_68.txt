Jennifer Klein, Thomas Burrow
Jennifer Allison Klein and Thomas Joseph Burrow were married Jan. 13 at San Francisco City Hall. Sarah Oswald, who became a Universal Life minister for the event, officiated.
Mrs. Burrow, 27, is an associate specializing in mergers and acquisitions in the Palo Alto, Calif., office of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom, the New York law firm. She graduated from Harvard, from which she also received a law degree.
She is the daughter of Diane D. Klein and Mathew D. Klein of Mesa, Ariz. The bride’s father retired as a plant manager at Composite Lining Systems, a company in Midland, Texas, that installs glass liners into new or used tubing and casing to guard against oil field corrosion damage. Her mother retired as a marketing coordinator for HNB Mortgage, a residential mortgage company in Midland.
Mr. Burrow, 28, is a vehicle engineer in Foster City, Calif., for Zoox, a company developing autonomous vehicles. He graduated from M.I.T.
He is a son of Ali K. Burrow and Rich S. Burrow of Suffield, Conn. His mother is a pharmacy technician at the Baystate Medical Center in Springfield, Mass. His father is retired as a systems engineer for Exelis, an aerospace and defense company in West Springfield, Mass.
The couple met during their senior year of college at an event thrown by the groom’s fraternity.
